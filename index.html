Felipe Resende Oliveira

Interesse

Graduated in Economics from the Federal University of Goiás (2012) with academic mobility from the University of Brasília (2011).
Master's degree (2014) and PhD (2018) in economics from the Postgraduate Program in Economics (PIMES) at the Federal University of Pernambuco.
He is currently a Professor at the Faculty of Economics and the Postgraduate Program in Economics at the Federal University of Mato Grosso.
He is a researcher in the Economic Research Area at the Economic and Socio-Environmental Research Center - NuPES/UFMT and supervising professor of the Project
Equity.info. His academic interests involve Economics of Education, Policy Evaluation and Competition Defense.
